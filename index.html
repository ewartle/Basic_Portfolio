About Me



My name is Lisa, and I am thrilled to be participating in the Georgia Tech Coding Boot Camp!

After nearly 10 years of practicing law, I decided to take life in a different direction and join the Peace Corps. I served as a Youth Development Volunteer in Morocco and created programming to promote learning, healthy lifestyles, and girl empowerment. I recently returned from Morocco, and I am motivated to learn new skills and explore ways that technology can bring about social change.

I am a reader, writer, tap dancer, and runner. I also enjoy traveling, spending time with friends and family, and making silly videos about my sister's pigs. Always, I love to laugh.
